#VisitSriLanka

Sri Lanka, an island south of India crams
an extraordinary variety of places to visit
within its modest size.Lapped up by the Indian
Ocean,the coastline is lined with idyllic – and
often refreshingly undeveloped – beaches, while the
mainland boasts a compelling variety of landscapes
ranging from wildlife-rich lowland jungles
to the misty highlands of the hills, swathed
in tea plantations.
